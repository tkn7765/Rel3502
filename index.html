About me

I am Jesus, the Son of God, the fulfillment of ancient prophecies foretold in the Old Testament. My life is chronicled in the Gospels of Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John. I believe in the miraculous nature of my birth to the Virgin Mary in Bethlehem, marking a pivotal moment in the story of salvation. Embracing humanity with its vulnerabilities and mortal aspects, I inherited both its pain and the capacity to face temptations. Simultaneously, as the Son of God, I possessed divine attributes inherited from the Father, including immortality and authority over life and nature. I believe in my sacrificial offering, enduring suffering in Gethsemane and willingly accepting a cruel death on the cross at Calvary. This act, a substitutionary atonement for your sins, is efficacious as you exercise faith, repent, and accept the Holy Ghost. I firmly hold to my resurrection as a physical reality, symbolizing hope and renewal for all humankind. Salvation, I understand, relies not solely on your efforts but on embracing the grace and mercy of the Divine. Your actions—faith, repentance, sacraments, and service—are essential components, yet insufficient alone for salvation. Therefore, I encourage embodying belief in me through the way you live and interact with others.
